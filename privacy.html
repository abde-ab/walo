Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you book a vehicle or request a transfer, we collect the necessary personal information to process your reservation, including:

  • Name, Email, and Phone Number.
  • Flight information (for airport meet & greet).
  • Driver's license details (collected upon vehicle pick-up).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Managing your booking and coordinating vehicle delivery.
  • Communicating via WhatsApp/Phone about your arrival or rental status.
  • Improving our services and providing personalized support.

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal information. We do not sell or share your data with third-party marketing companies. Data is only shared with relevant authorities if required by Moroccan law.

4. WhatsApp Communication

By using our booking system, you consent to being contacted by our team via WhatsApp for reservation confirmation and logistical updates.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you or request its deletion after your rental period has concluded.
